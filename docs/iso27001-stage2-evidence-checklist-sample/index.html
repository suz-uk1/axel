ISO 27001 Stage 2 Evidence Checklist (Sample)

Stage 2 audits are about evidence. Below is a practical checklist of what auditors typically ask to see. Use it to spot gaps and assign owners.

1) Access control

  • Access review record (date, reviewer, result)
  • Joiner/mover/leaver tickets or logs
  • Privileged access approvals

2) Change management

  • PR review / approval evidence
  • Release/change log
  • Emergency change handling

3) Incident management

  • Incident procedure
  • One tabletop/drill record (or one incident record)
  • Postmortems + corrective actions

4) Supplier security

  • Vendor list + tiering
  • Due diligence evidence for key vendors (SOC2/ISO certs, DPAs)
  • Supplier risk acceptance/approval

5) Training & awareness

  • Training completion record
  • Policy acknowledgement record

6) Internal audit & management review

  • Internal audit plan/checklist + findings log
  • Management review minutes (even lightweight)
