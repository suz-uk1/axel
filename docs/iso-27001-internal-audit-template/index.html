What auditors actually test

  • Not “do you have a policy?”
  • But “does reality match what your policy claims?”
  • And “can you show objective evidence consistently?”

Internal audit plan (recommended columns)

  1. Audit area / control theme
  2. Scope (system/team/process)
  3. Evidence to sample
  4. Sampling method (how many / which period)
  5. Auditor
  6. Date
  7. Findings summary
  8. Corrective action owner + due date

Evidence-first sampling checklist (fast)

  • Joiner/Mover/Leaver: access request → approval → provisioning → periodic review evidence
  • Change management: ticket/PR → approval → deploy → logs/monitoring
  • Backup & restore: restore test evidence (not just backup settings)
  • Supplier risk: 1 vendor end-to-end (selection → contract → monitoring)
  • Incidents: 1 incident record end-to-end (triage → comms → RCA → corrective action)
