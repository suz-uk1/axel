# How to use (20 minutes)

1) Put `incident-reporting-timeline.csv` into your shared drive (Sheets/Excel)
2) Add columns if your competent authority requires more detail
3) Define two roles:
   - Triage Owner (initial assessment)
   - Incident Commander (coordination)
4) Run a 15-minute tabletop:
   - pick a scenario (phishing, ransomware, SaaS compromise)
   - fill one row end-to-end
5) Save evidence:
   - ticket link
   - comms drafts
   - key timestamps

## What “T0” means
T0 is the moment your organisation becomes **aware** of a significant incident.

## Deliverable
After this, you can show:
- you track incident reporting deadlines
- you have assigned accountability
- you retain evidence
