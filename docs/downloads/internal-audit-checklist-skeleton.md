## 2) Checklist (high-level)

### A) ISMS basics
- [ ] Scope defined and approved
- [ ] Roles/responsibilities defined (ISMS owner, control owners)
- [ ] Document control/versioning in place

### B) Risk management
- [ ] Risk methodology documented
- [ ] Risk register maintained and reviewed on cadence
- [ ] Risk treatment actions tracked to completion

### C) Statement of Applicability (SoA)
- [ ] SoA exists and is current
- [ ] Applicability decisions are justified
- [ ] Evidence exists for implemented controls

### D) Operations evidence (examples)
- [ ] Access reviews performed and recorded
- [ ] Backups and restore tests recorded
- [ ] Incident log maintained
- [ ] Supplier reviews recorded
- [ ] Change approvals traceable (tickets/PRs)

### E) Improvement
- [ ] Nonconformities captured
- [ ] Corrective actions tracked
- [ ] Management review completed
