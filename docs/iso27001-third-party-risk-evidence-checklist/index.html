ISO 27001 Third-Party Risk Evidence Checklist

Use this checklist to prove supplier security due diligence in audits and enterprise procurement.

Free template: Evidence Map (CSV)

Evidence you need to keep

  1. Vendor inventory with risk tier
  2. Security review criteria (SOC2/ISO, DPA, breach history)
  3. Review results and approval decision
  4. Contract security clauses checklist
  5. Ongoing monitoring cadence and re-review date
  6. Exception log and mitigation actions

Fast response format

For each vendor: owner, risk tier, evidence links, last review date, next review date.
