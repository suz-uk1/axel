NIS2 readiness checklist (SME / supplier)

Use this to spot gaps in 5 minutes. If you score low, you don’t need a 50-page policy set tomorrow — you need 3 living artifacts: a risk register, an incident reporting timeline, and a supplier register.

Incident reporting guide (24h/72h) Get the €49 timeline kit

Governance

  • We have an owner accountable for cybersecurity risk management.
  • Management reviews top risks at least quarterly.
  • We keep evidence of approvals and reviews.

Risk management

  • We maintain a living risk register (likelihood × impact, owner, due date).
  • We track remediation actions to closure.

Incident response & reporting

  • We can define T0 and track deadlines (T0+24h / T0+72h).
  • We have an incident log with timestamps and evidence links.
  • We run at least one tabletop per year.

Supplier security

  • We have a supplier register with tiers (high/medium/low).
  • High-tier suppliers have documented security checks and contract clauses.

Technical basics (minimum)

  • MFA for critical systems is enforced.
  • Patching cadence exists and is tracked.
  • Backups are tested (restore proof exists).
