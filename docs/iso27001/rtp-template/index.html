Minimal fields (what auditors expect)

  • Risk ID (from risk register)
  • Risk statement (1 line)
  • Treatment decision (Mitigate / Accept / Transfer / Avoid)
  • Planned controls / actions (1–3 items)
  • Owner
  • Due date
  • Status
  • Evidence link(s)

How to keep it consistent

  • Every “Implemented” item has at least one evidence pointer.
