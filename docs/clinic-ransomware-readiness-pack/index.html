Clinic Ransomware Readiness Pack (APPI-friendly)

A practical kit of templates + printable checklists for small clinics (1–30 staff) to prepare for and respond to ransomware incidents.

Download (v0.2 ZIP) Back to Axel

Note: This is operational guidance and templates—not legal advice.

What’s inside

  • Printable ransomware incident checklist (first 60 min / 24h / 7d)
  • Staff one‑pager: ransomware & phishing basics
  • Suspected-incident flow (print & post)
  • Backup & restore policy template
  • Access control policy template
  • Vendor questionnaire (IT/EHR/backup providers)
  • Evidence & timeline worksheet

How to use (2–3 hours)

  1. Download the ZIP and save it to a shared clinic folder.
  2. Print the checklist + flowchart and place them where staff can find them.
  3. Send the vendor questionnaire to IT/EHR/backup providers.
  4. Run a 30-minute tabletop exercise (use the checklist).
