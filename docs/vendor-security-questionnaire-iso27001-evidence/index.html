Why questionnaires take so long

  • Answers live in people’s heads
  • Evidence is scattered across tools
  • No single owner can prove controls quickly

Buyers don’t need perfect prose — they need proof: owners, timestamps, and links.

What to prepare (the short list)

  • Access review record
  • Change approvals / release history
  • Incident drill record + postmortems
  • Supplier due diligence evidence (SOC2/ISO certs, DPAs)
  • Training completion evidence
  • Internal audit + management review evidence
